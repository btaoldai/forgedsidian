# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant**, version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct.

The full text is available at the official source:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

A plain-text translation in many languages is available at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations/

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (this repository, its issue tracker, its discussions, its pull requests, and any associated chat or community venues), and also when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Our pledge (summary)

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, and to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community. We commit to applying the standards described in the Contributor Covenant 2.1.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer through one of the following channels:

1. **GitHub private advisory** (recommended for any sensitive matter)
   https://github.com/btaoldai/forgedsidian/security/advisories/new

2. **GitHub** -- open a private channel by mentioning [@btaoldai](https://github.com/btaoldai) in a context where direct messaging is appropriate, or use the project's discussions for non-sensitive matters.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

For the full enforcement procedure, ladder, and response steps, refer to the official Contributor Covenant 2.1 text linked above.
